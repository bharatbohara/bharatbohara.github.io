Ph.D Research Projects

  • Deep Learning-based Framework for Automatic Analog Circuit Design and Analysis
    Funded by National Science Foundation
    • Developed a framework based on various deep-learning approaches to identify and auto-simulate hand-drawn schematics.
    • Achieved high component detection accuracy of mAP 96.7%, and the NetList generation accuracy of 95.71%.
    • Incorporated an automatic graph generation stage to obtain a graphical representation of those circuits’ topologies.
  • Prognostic Health Monitoring of Microgrids using Machine Learning Approaches
    Funded by National Science Foundation
    • Examined microgrid’s real-time health status by analyzing interaction between power converters at multiple nodes.
    • Utilized various machine learning approaches to identify and locate fault-prone zones within the convoluted grid network.
  • Short-term Load Forecasting using Short-term Long-term Memory (LSTM)
    Smart Grids - A course project
    • Developed bidirectional LSTM and CNN-BiLSTM to get 24-hour ahead load forecasts, given previous day’s load profile.
    • Reduced RMSE error for BiLSTM and CNN-BiLSTM by 5.6% and 2.85%, respectively, compared to the standard LSTM.
  • AI-powered Optimal Energy Management Tool for Resilient Microgrids
    UH Energy Innovation Commercialization Competition, Team AiGrid
    • Became top-5 finalist and got an opportunity to pitch our idea in the Chevron head office in Houston, TX.
    • Built an AI tool capable of learning and adapting to real-time data, predicting future energy demands.
    • The tool is optimized to find the most cost-effective and resilient management scenarios for the microgrid operators.

Other Projects

  • Model Predictive Control for Energy Systems in Buildings
    Masters Thesis
    • Designed an optimal predictive control system for maximizing energy savings while ensuring thermal comfort.
    • Implemented an adaptive MPC in Team Shunya House, a net-zero energy building built for the US-DOE Solar-Decathlon challenge, achieving energy savings of 7.06%.
  • LQR and MPC-based control for Single Board Multi-Heater System
    Advanced Processing Control - A course project
    • Designed real-time temperature control using LQR and MPC for a heater system, addressing measured and unmeasured environmental disturbances to solve servo and regulatory problems.
    • MPC performed better than LQR in both negating un/measured disturbances and tracking the targeted setpoint.
  • EnerCoin: a virtual currency concept for trading renewable energy in smart grids
    Energy system modeling and analysis - Poster Presentation
    • Developed a dynamic pricing algorithm for the competitive participation of consumers and prosumers in the open market.
    • A decentralized digital currency called EnerCoin was built on the Ethereum platform.
  • Techno-socioeconomic analysis of PV-wind hybrid power system
    Bachelors Degree Project
    • Conducted a comprehensive analysis of a PV-wind hybrid power system in a remote village in Nepal.
    • Observed the need of additional 6 kW PV system to meet the villagers’ water pump and grinding-mill systems demand.
    • Feasibility analyses conducted projected the viability of such hybrid systems in various regions across Nepal.
